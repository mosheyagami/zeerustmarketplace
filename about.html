About Zeerust Marketplace

Connecting communities and empowering local businesses

Our Mission

Zeeerust Marketplace was created to uplift and empower local businesses across South Africa. Our mission is to provide visibility, community connection, and digital tools to help local entrepreneurs thrive. We believe that a strong local economy is built on the success of its small businesses, and we are dedicated to being the platform that makes that success possible.

By bridging the gap between local consumers and service providers, we aim to foster a vibrant ecosystem where everyone benefits. Our platform offers a seamless way for people to discover hidden gems and for business owners to reach new customers, turning simple transactions into meaningful community relationships.

Our Story

Zeeerust Marketplace was born out of a simple idea: that every local business, no matter how small, deserves a chance to shine. As a passionate advocate for entrepreneurship, our founder, Goitsemodimo Mmutle, recognized the need for a dedicated digital space for South African businesses. The platform is a tribute to the resilience and innovation of local business owners.

“Empowering Mzansi, one listing at a time.” — Kgomane Goitsemodimo Mmutle

From the bustling markets of Johannesburg to the coastal towns of the Western Cape, we are committed to building a platform that reflects the diversity and spirit of South Africa.

What We Offer

  • Free Business Listings: We offer a free and easy way for any business to create a profile and be discovered by their community.
  • Authentic Reviews: Our community-driven review system helps users make informed decisions and provides valuable feedback to business owners.
  • Digital Tools: From photo galleries to direct contact options, we provide a suite of tools to help businesses manage their online presence.
  • Community Hub: More than just a directory, we are a hub for local events, discussions, and collaborations.
